For Arsh · on your birthday

Mere Bhai,

Kuch log life mein aate hain, aur kuch log life ka hissa ban jaate hain. Tu mere liye woh doosra wala hai.

You are the one who taught me more than you probably realise—how to stand on my own, how to think better, how to keep going, and how to show up for people without making a big deal out of it. Aaj jo bhi main hoon, usmein tera ek bahut bada part hai. I owe you, big time.

Maine tujhe struggle karte dekha hai. Scratch se apni life build karte dekha hai. Khud ke dum pe aage badhte dekha hai. Aur phir bhi, itna kuch carry karke, tune kabhi apne logon ko feel nahi hone diya ki they were a burden. That is the kind of man you are.

Tu emotions hide karta hai. Cool banne ki full koshish. But I know the truth: uss attitude aur intelligence ke peeche ek bahut bada, bahut soft heart hai. A heart that cares deeply, protects quietly, and loves without needing credit.

Thank you—har saal ke liye, meri har stupid baat sunne ke liye, kabhi judge na karne ke liye, aur har baar wahan hone ke liye jab it mattered. Tu best friend se zyada hai. Tu mera bhai hai.

You are the best friend anyone could ask for, the best bro, a wonderful son, and genuinely one of the best men I know. Please never change the parts of you that make you you.

Bas ek request hai: mere saath hamesha rehna. Life jitni bhi badle, cities kitni bhi door ho, years kitne bhi fast niklein—yeh rishta kabhi mat badalna.

Happy Birthday, Bhai. I hope life gives you back every bit of goodness you have quietly given everyone else.

Always proud of you,
tera bhai. ♥
